ABOUT ME

WHO AM I?

Despite the fact that my residence is in Kerala's Malappuram district, I am involved in a number of projects that have their headquarters in Malappuram district but may give services all over the world. My ambition is to attract new entrepreneurs and help them develop their businesses.

My development began at a very young age, when I expressed an interest in being an entrepreneur. I used to take advantage of business opportunities that were available to me when I was younger.If my memory serves me right, at the age of ten, I started trading candy for very young children and toddlers near home.

From there, I worked in numerous fields according to my age.Since it was study time (student) I did not expect any support from home.When I was a student I used to accumulate pocket money at home without anyone knowing, and became part of many low-cost businesses, or participated in the business of senior friends.

Like all fathers, my father, an expatriate, often advised me to concentrate on my studies. Yet my father did nothing to stop my comments and desires. Likewise, I would have received more support from the mothers of any son than anyone else.

I gained a lot of knowledge by running small businesses like mobile shop, cool bar, hotel, fancy, footwear, quail farm, broiler chicken farm, public service center, etc.

The truth is that i worked in many fields at a young age but was not able to achieve success. But I'm still proud of the fact that my quest to become an entrepreneur as I wished took place at a young age.

Since the ventures started during my school and college days, I have not been able to fully work on the projects that started during that time.Each of the ventures, which started during the summer vacation, was carried out by stabilizing the staff until the start of class after working only on vacation. The project will be discontinued or handed over to someone else when the study is over and can not be supervised at all.

I decided to try for a job after finishing my studies, probably because I felt that it was not enough to move on with life because of the unstable activities and all that could be started was small ventures.

I was interested in business and took up Commerce for Higher Secendary education.Then I did my B.Com Graduate from Calicut University and my MBA Post Graduate from Bharatiyar Univercity. Until the end of the Post Graduate study, the small projects mentioned above were on the sidelines.

When I decided to go abroad for work, all other plans were put on hold. Efforts for the job continued and as a precaution I set aside one year to study spoken English and gain work experience under a Charterd Accountant.

In 2017, I took a 3-month UAE visiting visa and returned to work without much interest.

Arriving abroad I had all the relatives and friends to help. Everyone helped me find work as much as they could and did other things. From there I got a small job and stayed at work for a few days. After seeing the lives of friends and relatives working abroad, I hated life in exile for a few days. Then came the thought of going back home and starting something of my own there. When it was time for my visiting visa to expire, I returned home.

I started learning about many ventures again. Since investment is an issue, I focused on what to start with low investment without thinking about big investment ventures.

When the locals and the housemates started harassing me by telling me about work, I immediately started a temporary venture. My purpose was to study various projects so I took a room and started an office. The office was on the 1st floor of a building in Othukkungal in Malappuram district. I started as an online service provider .I had a lot of time because the company was upstairs and no people came. In my spare time, I started uploading videos of my previous business ventures on YouTube. So I realized that a lot of friends want to start a business and people need a lot of knowledge.

That's how Dreambiz business solutions. Pvt. Ltd. came to be. Initially, it was intended to be a consulting firm covering all the services required by entrepreneurs .It's a training program to impart knowledge to those in need. Today, dreambiz has grown into a company with over 850 franchisees and present in every district in Kerala. In addition, my company has gained over 10,000 customers by providing other services.

Now I am on a journey to come up with many new projects that can mold new entrepreneurs like e-Mitra Seva Kendra. We also strive to grow in a way that will enable entrepreneurs to do whatever service they want.
